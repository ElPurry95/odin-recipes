Dynamite Shrimps

Description

Dynamite shrimp is a popular appetizer made with crispy battered shrimp tossed in a creamy, spicy sauce that usually combines mayonnaise, chili sauce, and a touch of sweetness

It is known for its crunchy texture and bold flavor, balancing heat, richness, and a slight tang in every bite.

Ingredients

  • Shrimp (peeled and deveined)
  • All-purpose flour
  • Baking powder
  • Mayonnaise
  • Sweet chili sauce
  • Sriracha sauce
  • Honey
  • Paprika
  • Garlic Powder
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Lemon Juice

Steps

  1. Peel and devein the shrimp, then pat them dry with paper towels.
  2. In a bowl, mix flour, cornstarch, baking powder, salt, black pepper, garlic powder, and paprika.
  3. Gradually add cold water to the dry ingredients and whisk until a smooth batter forms.
  4. Heat vegetable oil in a deep pan over medium-high heat for frying
  5. Dip each shrimp into the batter, making sure it is fully coated
  6. Fry the shrimp in hot oil until golden brown and crispy, then remove and place on paper towels to drain excess oil.
  7. n a separate bowl, combine mayonnaise, sweet chili sauce, sriracha, honey, and lemon juice to make the dynamite sauce.
  8. Toss the fried shrimp gently in the sauce until evenly coated.
  9. Garnish with chopped green onions and sesame seeds if desired, then serve immediately.
