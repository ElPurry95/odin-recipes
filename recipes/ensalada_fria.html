Ensalada Fria

Description

Based on a creamy pasta made with mayonnaise, peas, carrots, onions, ham and chicken.

It is served chilled and is especially popular at family gatherings, holidays, and celebrations in Cuba.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Ham
  • Chichen
  • Mayonnaise
  • Peas
  • Carrots
  • Onions
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta until tender, then let it cool completely.
  2. Boil the peas and carrots if they are not already cooked, and chop the onions finely.
  3. Cut the ham and chicken into small pieces.
  4. In a large bowl, combine the pasta with the peas, carrots, onions, and protein.
  5. Add mayonnaise, salt, and black pepper, then mix everything well until evenly coated.
  6. Refrigerate the salad for at least 1 hour before serving so it is cold and flavorful.
